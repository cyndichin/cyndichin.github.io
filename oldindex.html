Energy Sense

EnergySense is an iOS mobile application that balances automation and personal control of a light to reduce energy consumption to support users’ everyday experiences. EnergySense is built in iOS and with LittleBits sensors. My main responsibilities include interaction design, UI design and development, iOS.
